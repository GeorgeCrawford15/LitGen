    Quick start: free AI lit review

    1. Enter a topic in the search box.
    2. Open Settings (glowing pill) and paste a free API: Gemini or OpenAI (free tier).
    3. Add free database APIs: PubMed key, arXiv endpoint, Semantic Scholar.
    4. Save, then click Generate — we fetch papers, summarize, and extract Methods/Results/Data/Conclusions.
